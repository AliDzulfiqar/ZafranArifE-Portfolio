About Zafran


Zafran is a young mathematician from Washington State University. He has established himself as a prominent leader, consistently demonstrating exceptional leadership skills by spearheading multiple organizations, including being the Secretary-General of the Overseas Indonesian Students' Association Alliance (OISAA/PPI Dunia).

Beyond his leadership achievements, Zafran is a passionate digital literacy enthusiast. He exhibits a profound commitment to the fields of data science and machine learning. His dedication to these disciplines is underscored by his drive to harness data-driven insights for real-world applications, showcasing his forward-thinking and problem-solving abilities.

With a rare combination of leadership acumen, mathematical prowess, and a passion for data science, Zafran is poised to continue making significant contributions to the academic and professional arenas. He is a promising talent whose innovative thinking and commitment to excellence make him a standout figure in the Mathematics field.
